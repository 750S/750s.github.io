About Us

750Sparks is a robotics team part of the South Brunsiwck High School robotics program. We have built a robot to participate in the 2019-2020 VEX Robotics competition, Tower Takeover. Thanks to the Robotics Parents Association This was the first year we were a team. We are proud to say we made it all the way to the New Jersey State championships. We will see you at worlds next year!
